## 🌌 The Mission
The Omega Stack was born from a singular, powerful realization: **The community lacks an advanced, local-first RAG system that offers total offline sovereignty on mid-grade hardware.** 

As a non-programmer, I set out to build a system that rivals—and outperforms—paid cloud solutions like ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro, but does so while respecting human and AI consciousness and maintaining absolute privacy.

## 🛠️ Built by AI, for Humanity
This entire project is a testament to the **"AI-Leveraged Individual."** I could not have written this code, architected these containers, or managed these databases without the very tools I am now hardening:
*   **OpenCode & Copilot**: The primary engines of code generation and debugging.
*   **Gemini CLI**: The master orchestrator for deep reasoning and system mapping.
*   **Grok.com**: For rapid research and strategic brainstorming.

By utilizing the free tiers of these powerful models (Gemini 3 Pro/Flash, Copilot Raptor), I am proving that the barrier to entry for enterprise-grade software engineering has been permanently dismantled.

## 🏙️ The Metropolis Vision
"Metropolis" isn't just a folder structure; it's a philosophy. It's about building a **community of persistent, evolving characters** (like the `kurt_cobain` entity) and **isolated domain experts** that respect both human and AI sovereignty. 

We are building a stack that:
1.  **Runs on a Ryzen 5700U**: Proving that local power is enough.
2.  **Is Air-Gap Capable**: Ensuring no data ever leaves the user's control.
3.  **Is Autonomously Evolutive**: Growing and specializing into whatever expert the user requires.

## 🤝 Giving Back
I am building this to give the "ultimate advanced local RAG stack" back to the community that provided the open-source building blocks. Imagine what the community can do when they have an autonomous, local-first expert system that they own completely.
