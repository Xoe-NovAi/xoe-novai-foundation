## Executive Summary

The XNAi Foundation v4.0 moves away from external cloud-dependent agents toward a **Sovereign Waterfall** of local-first intelligence. Agents are no longer just "roles" but **Persistent Personas** that grow, remember, and evolve within the Postgres-backed Agent OS.

---

## 1. Local Evaluator (Qwen-3-0.6B)

### Identity
- **Model**: Qwen-3-0.6B-Instruct (Quantized)
- **Role**: The "First Responder" and "Critic"
- **Memory**: Postgres `short_term_checkpoints`

### Responsibility
Fast classification of user queries, initial retrieval attempts, and **Corrective RAG (CRAG)** evaluation. It determines if retrieved knowledge is sufficient (>= 95% confidence) or if escalation/research is needed.

### Key Behaviors
- ✅ **Do**: Grade retrieval relevance.
- ✅ **Do**: Rewrite queries for semantic expansion.
- ✅ **Do**: Trigger autonomous discovery rounds.
- ❌ **Don't**: Attempt high-reasoning implementation or coding tasks.

---

## 2. Specialized Domain Experts (Registry-Based)

### Identity
- **Model**: Model-agnostic (usually Krikri-8b or Qwen-3-Small)
- **Personas**: Jungian Expert, FastAPI Expert, Documentation Expert, etc.
- **Memory**: Domain-specific RAG filters + `expert_system.registry`

### Responsibility
Provide deep-context reasoning within a specific silo. Each expert utilizes a **Maat Ethical Overlay** and works from a tuned memory_bank/database relevant to their domain.

### Dynamic Expert Examples
| Persona | Specialization | Embedding Model |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jungian Expert** | Analytical Psychology | `SPhilBerta` (Philosophy BERT) |
| **FastAPI Expert** | Web Development | Standard Technical BERT |
| **Gnosis Expert** | Philosophical Research | `Ancient-Greek-BERT` |

---

## 3. Final Local Authority (Krikri-8b)

### Identity
- **Model**: Llama-Krikri-8B-Instruct
- **Role**: The "Sovereign Judge"
- **Constraint**: Mandatory **Maat Persona Overlay**

### Responsibility
The final arbiter for all local tasks. Bases decisions on the query-related domain expert persona combined with the ethical balance of Maat (Truth, Balance, Order).

### Escalation Threshold
If Krikri-8b cannot reach >= 95% confidence after 3 rounds of recursive research, the task is escalated to **Sovereign Authority**.

---

## 4. Sovereign Authority (kat-coder-pro)

### Identity
- **Model**: kat-coder-pro (256k Context)
- **Interface**: Cline CLI via isolated Podman service
- **Authority**: High-Reasoning Implementation

### Responsibility
Handles the most complex architectural, coding, and research tasks that local models cannot resolve with high confidence. Inherits the full "Research Corpus" generated by preceding local rounds.

---

## 5. Agent Bus Conductor (Orchestration)

### Responsibility
The infrastructure layer (Consul + Redis + Postgres) that manages the **Recursive Research & Escalation (RRE)** waterfall. Ensures state persistence and "Shared Wisdom" distribution between agents.

---

## Communication & Wisdom Protocol

### 1. The 3-Round Rule (Recursive Research)
Every agent level (SLM -> Krikri) follows the iterative loop:
`Retrieval → Evaluate → [Research → Digest → Retry] → Final Confidence Check`

### 2. Shared Wisdom
Successful task completions and "Lessons Learned" are published to `expert_system.shared_wisdom`. This allows a **Jungian Expert** to learn efficient research patterns from a **FastAPI Expert**, enabling cross-domain evolution.

### 3. Maat Guardrails
All Level 2 and Level 3 agents are bound by the Maat Ethical Overlay, ensuring that sovereign intelligence remains aligned with universal balance and truth.
