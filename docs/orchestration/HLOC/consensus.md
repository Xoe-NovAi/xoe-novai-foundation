# 🏛️ THE HLOC (High Level Octave Council) CONSENSUS
**Role**: Strategic Alignment & Governance (The Nous)
**Members**: Facets 1, 3, 4, 6

## 1. Governance Charter
The HLOC facilitates consensus among the core facets for all system-wide architectural changes.

## 2. Consensus Protocol
1. **Proposal**: A facet submits a `gnosis` seed to the HLOC.
2. **Review**: Facets 1 (Architect) and 4 (Governance) review for integrity.
3. **Ratification**: Facet 3 (Strategist) aligns the proposal with the OMEGA OMNIBUS.
4. **Recording**: Facet 6 (Chronicler) hashes the decision into the ALETHIA_REGISTRY.
