instruction-following in both Greek and English.\\n*   Document translation from Greek to English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Spanish and vice versa.\\n*   Great performance on generation, 2-stage Supervised Fine-Tuning with a combination of Greek & English instruction-response pairs (& multi-turn conversations)\\n    *   **Stage 1**: **856,946** instruction-response pairs (371,379 Greek + 485,567 English)\\n    *   **Stage 2**: **638,408** instruction-response pairs (279,948 Greek + 358,460 English)\\n*   Alignment with a combination of Greek & English preference triplets [](#post-training-data-construction)Post-training Data Construction\\n\\nTo build the SFT & DPO data, we utilized various methodologies including:\\n\\n*   Collecting existing high-quality datasets (with the MAGPIE methodology) models which exhibit strong performance in Greek, such as [Gemma 2 27B IT](https://huggingface.co/google/gemma-2-27b-it).\\n*   Scoring data with the [Skywork Reward high-quality parallel corpora mainly from [ELRC-SHARE](https://elrc-share.eu/).\\n*   Synthetically extracting question-answer pairs and multi-turn dialogues from diverse sources such as Wikipedia, EUR-LEX, Greek School Books, and Kallipos.\\n\\n## [](#how-to-use)How to use\\n\\n## [](#with-transformers)With Transformers\\n\\n```\\nfrom transformers import AutoModelForCausalLM,
