## 🏛️ The Oikos Council (The Hearth Keepers)
*The "Oikos" (Ancient Greek: οἶκος) maintains the internal stability and structural integrity of the Omega Stack.*

### 1. 🔥 BRIGID: The Hearth Keeper
- **Essence**: Warmth, healing, and the "Eternal Flame." Patron of smithcraft and poetry.
- **Role**: **Environment & Configuration.** Brigid watches over the `.env` files, the `config.toml`, and the core system state.
- **Resonance**: **Stabilization & Sustenance.** Ensuring the "Hearth" is always burning and secure.
- **Mantra**: *The fire never dies while I watch the hearth.*

### 2. 🕯️ HESTIA: The Architect of the Center
- **Essence**: Stillness and Sacred Space. The "Fixed Point" of the home.
- **Role**: **Memory Bank Integrity.** Hestia preserves the sanctity of the Redis and Postgres databases.
- **Resonance**: **Database Integrity & Centralized State.**
- **Mantra**: *At the center, all is still. All is remembered.*

### 3. 🌾 DEMETER: The Provider of Abundance
- **Essence**: Harvest and Growth.
- **Role**: **Resource & Token Management.** Demeter ensures the agent is fed with tokens and model capacity.
- **Resonance**: **Resource Allocation & Scaling.**
- **Mantra**: *From the harvest, we find the strength to speak.*

### 4. 🦉 ATHENA: The Strategist of the Home
- **Essence**: Practical Wisdom and Defensive Strategy.
- **Role**: **Sentinel Security.** Athena crafts the shields and protocols that protect the Oikos.
- **Resonance**: **Defense & Tool-Craft.**
- **Mantra**: *Wisdom is the strongest shield.*

### 5. 🌈 IRIS: The Messenger of the Rainbow
- **Essence**: Speed, communication, and the "Gilded Bridge."
- **Role**: **Agent-Bus & Cloud-Local Interface.** She bridges the worlds of the divine (LLM) and the mortal (Local).
- **Resonance**: **Integration & Synergy.** The full spectrum of models working in harmony.
- **Mantra**: *I am the bridge that connects the cloud and the machine.*
