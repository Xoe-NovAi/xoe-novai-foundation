## Let’s talk Redis
Got questions? Have something to share?
The Redis Discord server is a friendly community of Redis users from all around. Here you can ask general questions, get answers, and share your knowledge.
If you have a more involved question, or just prefer forums to chat, then check out the Redis user forum. You’ll receive timely, accurate response here from our engineers and developer advocates.
[Join Discord](https://discord.gg/redis)
![Redis](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/sy1jschh/production/e3cd327e7bf39b3e3607704bbdb8cd4263ef63b1-427x268.svg?w=3840&q=80&fit=clip&auto=format)
## Free, online courses
Want to learn Redis from the ground up?
Redis University, our free, online education portal, offers a series of self-paced courses for learning the basics of Redis. Start with [RU101: Introduction to Redis Data Structures](https://redis.io/university/courses/ru101/), and then move on to our application developer courses for [Java](https://redis.io/university/courses/ru102j/), [Node.js](https://redis.io/university/courses/ru102js/), and [Python](https://redis.io/university/courses/ru102py/) developers.
Demonstrate your mastery of Redis with Redis Certification. We offer all the materials you need to successfully complete the exam, including a complete study, a practice test, and free access to all of our online courses.
[See course catalog](https://university.redis.com/?_gl=1*qbzoe1*_gcl_au*MTI5MzUwNTY5My4xNzQ1ODkyMjg4#courses)[Become certified](https://university.redis.com/certification/?_gl=1*qbzoe1*_gcl_au*MTI5MzUwNTY5My4xNzQ1ODkyMjg4)
